About Strikers Installation

Strikers Installation is an Otago/Southland based Hardware Installation Specialist. With a large range of mortising equipment, we have everything you need to suit any project Big or small ranging from large Commercial through to High End residential builds. We offer a friendly and efficient service and can supply hardware or install hardware purchased through other suppliers. We also specialise in installation of accessible bathroom hardware and dispensers.

As a proud Sitesafe member and SiteWise Green Certified company we value our team at Strikers Installation and take pride in our high safety standards For more information on our services, or advice on selecting or purchasing the right products please contact us on 02108065547 or Tyson@strikersinstallation.com
